### Welcome to my website!

I've redone my website for like the third time now. This website should be much easier to navigate (I had a right sided navigation bar for some reason on previous one lol).

I originally was just going to use the [Academic Pages](https://github.com/academicpages/academicpages.github.io) website template rather try to fix my old website.

Most people who use that template are PhD students or even professors (my prof who I did research with at Purdue uses it), but I only recently graduated from my Bachelor's and have no publications to my name.

I hope to get more involved in research in my Master's, and will hopefully have something to show soon.

My previous website does have a really cool theme with the high contrast and neon colors, so I decided to update it to have a cleaner interface and display updated and more relevant information about me. I wanted to take aspects of that template but still show my style and personality through my personal website.

### How I made my website

This website is made with `Vue.js`, `Vite`, and deployed via `Vercel`. I also used `TailwindCSS` for the styling. One thing I added was rendering markdown files using `TailwindCSS Typography` and a markdown parser. This makes writing blog posts really convenient and the tailwind typography plugin is legit insane (I can style literally every single markdown feature).
